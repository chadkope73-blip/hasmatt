HasMatt

If there is a danger nearby, this app will let you know. Live hazard awareness for travelers, field workers, campers, and people who want a fast read on nearby risks before they settle in.

Live scan sources: weather alerts, recent earthquakes, EPA Superfund sites, and current interagency wildfire perimeters.
This version is online-first and pulls live public hazard data when available. Always use judgment and follow official local guidance.
